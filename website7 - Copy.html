Some short notes on geology:


Exogeneous geological agent :

In geology, exogenous processes come from forces on or above the Earth's surface. Processes caused by forces from within the Earth are endogenous processes. Exo is a prefix meaning "out", and endo is a prefix meaning "in". Many exogenous (extraterrestrial) forces are caused by other bodies in the Solar System.
